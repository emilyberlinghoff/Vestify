\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Scores stocks on financial quality and balance sheet health. 

Evaluates four quality dimensions:\+
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Current ratio (ability to meet short-\/term obligations)
\item Debt-\/to-\/equity (lower leverage is safer)
\item Gross margin (pricing power and competitive advantage)
\item ROE (management effectiveness, capped to avoid distortion)
\end{DoxyItemize}

Each component maps to a 0-\/100 sub-\/score, then averaged. This model complements valuation and growth screens by emphasizing durable balance-\/sheet strength and profitability quality.
