\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Scores stocks on capital efficiency and operational performance. 

Evaluates three efficiency dimensions:\+
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item ROA (how well the company uses its assets to generate profit)
\item Operating margin (operational efficiency before interest/\+tax)
\item EV/\+\+EBIT (market valuation relative to operating earnings)
\end{DoxyItemize}

Each component maps to a 0-\/100 sub-\/score, then averaged. It highlights businesses that turn assets and operations into profit efficiently without requiring excessive valuation support.
